I have been contracted by Fred the Baker Baking Company to take a concept, Donut Maker Clicker, from the drawing board to the browser. Fred the Baker is tired of making real donuts and is ready to retire. He needed help to keep his love of donuts alive. He wanted me to create a game that uses button clicks to create virtual donuts for the world to enjoy. It's always "Time to make the Donuts" and Fred is too busy to give us guidance on how to implement these requirements. Luckily I have made this and it is fully functional.
